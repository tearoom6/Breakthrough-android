Memorize an order of panels flushing, and trace in that order. It's simple!
<font color="red">Simple, but exciting!</font> You must be addicted to this game!

<b>How to play</b>
It's simple. First, panels flush randomly.
<font color="red">Memorize an order of flushing, then touch them in that order.</font>
you can touch panels one by one, <font color="red">but it's recommended to trace panels keeping finger touched with screen.</font>

<font color="red">Each stages have limitation time. Please be careful!</font> More rest time you keep, more bonus points you would get.
As game proceeded, a variety of stages are waiting you! For example, touch with 1-skip or touch in reverse order.

Besides, this game has two modes.
・<font color="red">Quest Mode</font>
・<font color="red">Challenge Mode</font>
Quest mode completes in 15 stages. When completed, you can see something new!?
In challenge mode, you can compete with in the world ranking!
